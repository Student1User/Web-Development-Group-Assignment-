Our Story

Code & Connect was founded in 2015 by a group of passionate technologists who shared a common vision: to make cutting-edge technology accessible to businesses of all sizes.

What began as a small team of five developers working out of a garage has grown into a global technology powerhouse with over 200 employees. Throughout our journey, we've remained true to our founding principles of innovation, quality, and client satisfaction.

Today, Code & Connect stands at the forefront of digital transformation, helping organizations navigate the complex technological landscape and leverage the power of emerging technologies to drive growth and create value.
